Is my meal plan any good?
As long as it meets your calorie and macronutrient needs and doesn’t look like it was put together by a five-to-sixteen year old whose parents are out of town, your meal plan is fine. Specific food choices make no noticeable difference provided you aren’t just stuffing your face with junk food. You can read a bit more about this in the article “

” by Lyle McDonald (10 min read), as well as some tangentially related information in our own extensively researched
